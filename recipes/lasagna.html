Lasagna

Description

This is an easy lasagna recipe that anyone can make!

Servings: 7

Yield: 6 to 8 servings

Ingredients

  • Lean ground beef, 1lb.
  • Onion, chopped, x1
  • Green bell pepper, chopped, x1
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Tomato paste, 12oz (x2 6oz cans)
  • Water, 2.25 cups
  • Ricotta cheese, part skim, 1 pint
  • Egg, x1 large
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded, 2 cups
  • Lasagna noodles, cooked and drained, x8

Steps

  1. Brown meet with onions and bell peppers, add season to taste. Add tomato paste and water. Let simmer.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mix ricotta chees with beaten egg. Set aside.
  3. Preheat oven to 370 degrees F (190 deg. C). Butter a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  4. Begin layering all ingredients beginning with a few spoonfuls of tomato sauce. Follow with noodles, then ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella. Repeat until dish is filled.
  5. Bake at 375 degrees F (190 deg. C) about 35 to 45 minutes, until bubbly. Let cool a couple of minutes before cutting.
  6. Plate and serve.
